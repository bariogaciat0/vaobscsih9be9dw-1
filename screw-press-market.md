<p><strong>The Screw Press Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Press</li><li>Twin Screw Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw press market is primarily divided into two types: single screw press and twin screw press. Single screw presses utilize a single rotating screw to process materials, making them ideal for simpler applications with lower production rates. In contrast, twin screw presses feature two intermeshed screws that enable more complex processing, offering better mixing and higher throughput. Both types serve various industries, including food, plastics, and biomass, catering to specific processing needs and product characteristics.</p></p>
